Why Cheshire Businesses Choose Ensign Manufacturing

Cheshire is home to a diverse mix of chemical processing, food manufacturing, logistics and engineering businesses — particularly along the M56 and M6 corridors through Warrington, Runcorn, Northwich and Ellesmere Port. These industries regularly require robust industrial components that meet strict safety and performance standards.

Ensign Manufacturing supplies fabricated slide gate valves to process plants across Cheshire, where precise flow control of powders, granules and bulk solids is essential. Our steel inspection doors are used by HVAC and ductwork contractors throughout the county, while our spill containment pallets help businesses in Cheshire meet Environment Agency and COSHH compliance requirements.

We serve clients across all of Cheshire including Chester, Crewe, Macclesfield, Wilmslow, Knutsford, Northwich, Warrington, Runcorn and Ellesmere Port. Speak to our team today for product advice and a fast quote.
