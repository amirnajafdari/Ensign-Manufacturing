Why Huddersfield Businesses Choose Ensign Manufacturing

Huddersfield has a long heritage of heavy industry, manufacturing and engineering. From the textile mills of the 19th century to today's advanced manufacturing businesses in Kirklees, industrial components need to be built to last. Ensign Manufacturing understands these demands and supplies products that perform reliably in demanding environments.

We supply fabricated slide gate valves and diverter valves to processing plants across West Yorkshire, including businesses operating in and around Huddersfield, Dewsbury, Brighouse, and Halifax. Our steel inspection doors are specified by engineers for duct access in industrial plants throughout the region, while our spill containment pallets help companies meet UK environmental compliance requirements.

Based in Worksop, we are well-placed to deliver to Huddersfield quickly and efficiently. Whether you need a single unit or a large batch order, our team can advise on the right product for your application.
